Welcome to my travel page


I didn't start exploring the world until my 20s.
But I sure made up for lost travel time!

Growing up, I spent every summer holiday traveling around the UK with my mum.
I didn't get on a plane until I was twelve years old!
My first holiday was third-wheeling my parents on thier honeymoon to Dubai.

During my younger years, Scotland became a regular summer retreat during the holidays.
This beautiful place is still one of my favourite places to go for a long weekend.


What ever you are looking for, Scotland has it

If you are looking for breathtaking nature trails, there are so many beautiful spots to choose from.

Whereas, I often visit Edinburgh, which has an abundance of history, culture, good food and many bars. Click here for more recommendations like this.


I have not always had the luxury of
travelling abroad, since holidays cost!


After I graduated from university, I was lucky to land my first job working as an air stewardess.
From this moment onwards, my travel life 'took off!.' Pun intended.

Cabin crew really changed my life. I realised there is so much of the world to see, explore and experience.




Now, earlier I mentioned that travel costs money and I haven't always had the luxury to travel.

Mentioning this now is intentional. I no longer work as a flight attendant.

I decided to stop during the corona virus outbreak to see what else life has to offer.

But this, doesn't mean I have stopped travelling. I travel more now, and for longer periods of time. I just do it on a budget.

Affordable travel is so easy and I can share my tips with you! Travel is good for the soul, broadens your outlook on life and is an amazing way to meet new friends along the way.

Happy Travels!
Love Jade
